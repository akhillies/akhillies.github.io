Coding Graphics

With coding and graphics, many things are possible. How do you think video games work? It is all about the user inputs and the code's interpretation of that input. Movies use many concepts within coding graphics. Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, AutoDesk Maya and AutoCAD, and even open source programs like Blender and Gimp. How do these amazing programs work behind the scenes? How do they adapt the image or structure such that the result is something so fluid and amazing? Well the basics of that is what I explored in these projects. Remember, these are barely the surface of what all can be done with code and graphics!
